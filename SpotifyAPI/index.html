YOUR Stats.

Welcome to Spotistics, where you can explore your personal music journey! Discover your top tracks, favorite artists, and most-loved genres from the past. Relive the highlights of your listening history and see how your tastes have evolved over time.

How To Use

Simply log in with your Spotify credentials to explore your personalized music statistics. View your top artists or tracks, and choose from three time ranges: the past month, the last 6 months, or all time. Discover trends in your listening habits and enjoy insights into your favorite music over time.

Switch views on the left side bar to view your home, top artists, top tracks, or top genres.

Change time frame on the top bar to view your statistics in the past month, 6 months, or all-time.
