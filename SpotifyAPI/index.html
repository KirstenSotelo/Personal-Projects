YOUR Stats.

Welcome to Spotistics, where you can explore your personal music journey! Discover your top tracks, favorite artists, and most-loved genres from the past. Relive the highlights of your listening history and see how your tastes have evolved over time.

How To Use

Simply log in with your Spotify credentials to explore your personalized music statistics. View your top artists or tracks, and choose from three time ranges: the past month, the last 6 months, or all time. Discover trends in your listening habits and enjoy insights into your favorite music over time.

Switch views on the left side bar to view your home, top artists, top tracks, or top genres.

Change time frame on the top bar to view your statistics in the past month, 6 months, or all-time.

Privacy Policy

Data Collection

We use Spotify authentication to access your data. This process allows us to retrieve information about your Spotify account, specifically your played tracks.

Data Usage

The data we collect is solely used to display your Spotify listening statistics, such as top tracks and artists. We do not modify, store, or use your data for any other purposes.

Data Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose your data to any third parties. Your information remains private and is not provided to any external organizations or individuals.

Purpose

This website serves only as a platform to display your Spotify statistics. No additional processing or data handling occurs beyond what is necessary to present your listening history.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to ensure that your data is accessed securely through Spotify's authentication system. However, as this website is intended purely for display purposes, no sensitive data is stored.
